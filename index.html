Isiolo County Youth Innovation Center

Isiolo County innovation center is a dynamic space designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration among young individuals. It provides resources, workshops, and mentorship to nurture innovative ideas and projects.

WFP Empact Digital Freelancing

Empact is a program by WFP innovation it focuses on Nurturing digital freelancers through comprehensive training, equipping them with skills, strategies, and confidence for online success.Youths are introduced to online working platforms where they can find jobs

  • Remotask is one of the online working platform where the youths will be guided and mentored by empact allumni technical mentors on how one can get online remote jobs after opening an account and build their profiles and work over time for them to be in a good position to acquire online remote jobs.

nomadsCode software school

nomadsCode is Empowering Isiolo youth with web development skills through a transformative software boot camp experience.We mentor and train youth on:

  • Front end Technologies [HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, ANGULAR,].
  • Back end Technologies [PYTHON, DJANGO, POSTGRESQL].
  • Programming languages and Coding [JAVASCRIPT, PYTHON].
  • Personal portfolio Branding and professional soft skills.

Office Computer Packages

Empowering Isiolo's youth with computer skills through a comprehensive training package at the Isiolo Youth Innovation Centre. The packages offered are:

  • Microsoft word , Excel, Access and Database, Power Point
  • Professionalism in Office Packages.

ART, Tailoring & Design, Culture, Entertainment

Isiolo Youth Innovation Center Empowers tomorrow's leaders through diverse programs in ART, Tailoring & Fashion Design, Culture, Entertainment :

  • Unleash creativity with art
  • Master music as a DJ.
  • Craft fashion through tailoring.
  • Celebrate heritage with cultural engagement and Cultural Artefact Design.
